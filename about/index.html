About

Hi! My name is Aju. I am a Software Engineer and has worked on tech domains like web development, game development, workflow automations, CLI tool building, image processing and more. I have also worked as a maths teacher/content developer and also led a team who was incharge of Visual Content Communication.

I am fueled by my passion for understanding and learning about tech. Mostly about computer software. I’ll always consider myself as a student and I try to learn whenever possible.

Something that I’m proud of myself is my ability to learn and understand a workflow. I always look at things in an analytical point of view. I try to optimize and automate stuff because I love it. I always try to make work easier for my teammates by automating repetitive tasks.

Other than tech stuff, my interests are pencil sketching, music production, origami and singing.
